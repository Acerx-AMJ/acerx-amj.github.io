About me and this website

Development

I originally started making this site on January 24th for two reasons - to show off all of my projects including private ones and to start writing blogs - both haven't changed since. I sketched up the site quite quickly and began styling it with Tailwind CSS. It was my first time using it and I enjoyed it. Until I realised I had to copy monstrous amounts of classes from one <p> tag to another. I moved back to regular CSS, put it all into a single file and kept the font and the colors the same. Works well.

I also originally had used JQuery to load the footer and the header dynamically but I abandoned it as well solely because it took too long to load it and the site would flicker. And at the start, the website used Typescript but it was too much for a project of a scale so minuscule. After abandoning these things I went back to the basics and used pure HTML/CSS/JS. I feel like these three things were only slowing me down here. Okay, they definitely are genuinely helpful for bigger projects but this just isn't one.

I really like how it turned out. I'm just into simple things. I think it's a good way of finding out more about myself since I haven't published some of the projects listed here anywhere else. I just hope I'll find some time and ideas in the future to write more blogs.

I don't think there's anything more to say about this website, just take a look around and see for yourself!
